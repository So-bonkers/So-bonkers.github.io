The Education Angle

Your real differentiator

Other DAWs show you what you did. BeatML shows you what it means, where it came from, and what else you can do with it.

🎵

Scale & Note Detection

Detects your scale, names your notes, and tells you exactly why your chord progression sounds the way it does.

"That's a flat VII chord — it gives the resolution that sense of landing without fully closing."

🌍

World Music Context

10,000+ real examples across 80+ cultures. Every creative decision you make is mapped to its global musical ancestry.

"This pentatonic pattern appears in West African kora music, Appalachian folk, and modern K-pop."

🎛️

Genre Mode

Tells you what trap, lo-fi, afrobeats, and jazz producers actually do differently — arrangement, timing, texture, harmony.

"Trap sidesteps clean harmony almost entirely. The 808 IS the bass line AND the melody."

🔗

Chord Suggestions with Reasoning

Suggests next chords and shows the music theory behind each one — not hidden in a tooltip, front and centre.

"Try a ii–V–I here. It creates tension then resolution — the most common move in jazz for 80 years."

Technical Credibility

For the producer who needs convincing

Under the hood it's a serious piece of software. Here's what it actually does.

AI Stem SeparationLocal

Drop any song. Pull out drums, bass, and vocals as separate stems — all processed on your machine, no upload required.

MIDI Editor + AI Quantisation

Full MIDI editing with AI-assisted quantisation and pattern variations. Keeps your groove, fixes the timing.

Hardware-Aware InferenceAdaptive

Automatically selects the right model size for your machine. Runs fast on modest hardware; runs better when you have it.

Open .beatml Format

Your sessions are stored in an open, documented format. Read them, move them, convert them — no lock-in, ever.
