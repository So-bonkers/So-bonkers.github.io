The Education Angle

Your real differentiator

Other DAWs show you what you did. EchoForge shows you what it means, where it came from, and what else you can do with it.

Scale & Note Detection

Detects your scale, names your notes, and tells you exactly why your chord progression sounds the way it does.

"That's a flat VII chord — it gives the resolution that sense of landing without fully closing."

🌍

World Music Context

10,000+ real examples across 80+ cultures. Every creative decision mapped to its global musical ancestry.

"This pentatonic pattern appears in West African kora music, Appalachian folk, and modern K-pop."

🎛️

Genre Mode

Tells you what trap, lo-fi, afrobeats, and jazz producers actually do differently — arrangement, timing, texture, harmony.

"Trap sidesteps clean harmony almost entirely. The 808 IS the bass line AND the melody."

🔗

Chord Suggestions with Reasoning

Suggests next chords and shows the music theory behind each one — front and centre, not hidden in a tooltip.

"Try a ii–V–I here. It creates tension then resolution — the most common move in jazz for 80 years."

Technical Credibility

MIDI Editor + AI Quantisation

"Music theory was never supposed to be gatekeeping."

Every producer who learned to make music without formal training had to reverse-engineer the theory from the records they loved. That's beautiful — but it's also slow, lonely, and full of false starts. EchoForge is the tool that should have existed already: one that sits next to you and explains what your instincts are doing, in the language of the music you actually listen to.
